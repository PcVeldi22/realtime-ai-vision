How it works

NeuroVision combines two on-device AI models: COCO-SSD for general object detection, and Tesseract.js for reading printed text on packaging. Both run fully client-side — nothing is ever uploaded to a server.

Note: COCO-SSD recognizes 80 general categories (person, bottle, book, etc.) and has no concept of specific branded products. The OCR panel helps bridge that gap by reading the actual text on a label, when it's clearly visible.
